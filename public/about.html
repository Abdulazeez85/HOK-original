His Story

Proving That Academics & Business Can Coexist

Adeyemi started HOK Computers while studying Medicine at the University of Ilorin. Where most students were focused purely on academics, he saw an opportunity — Ilorin had a growing demand for quality tech products but lacked a trustworthy, accessible local source.

He registered the business with the CAC in 2020, started small, and built it on reputation — one device, one satisfied customer at a time. Today HOK Computers has served 200+ clients and sold over 500 devices across Kwara State.

His vision: merge technology and healthcare innovation. HOK Computers is just the beginning.
